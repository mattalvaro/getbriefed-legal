Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new person?

Tap the "+" button on the People tab to add a new contact. You can add their name, company, role, and photo.

What are Facts?

Facts are memorable details about a person - things you'd be embarrassed to forget. Add facts like "Has a daughter named Emma" or "Big skier, goes to Tahoe every February."

How does iCloud sync work?

GetBriefed automatically syncs your data across all your devices using iCloud. Make sure you're signed into the same iCloud account on all devices and that iCloud is enabled for the app.

What's included in the free version?

The free version lets you track up to 10 people with unlimited facts and interactions. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited people, voice notes, and relationship health insights.

How do I restore my subscription?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases." This will restore any active subscriptions associated with your Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device to manage or cancel your subscription.
