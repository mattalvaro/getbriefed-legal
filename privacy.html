Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

Summary: GetBriefed stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal information on external servers.

Introduction

GetBriefed ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. GetBriefed is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • Local Storage Only: All data you enter (contacts, facts, interactions, notes) is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's secure on-device storage.
  • No Account Required: You do not need to create an account or provide any personal information to use GetBriefed.
  • No Analytics: We do not use analytics or tracking tools to monitor your usage.
  • No Third-Party Data Sharing: Your data is never shared with third parties.

AI-Powered Features

GetBriefed offers optional AI-powered features to generate conversation topics. When you use these features:

  • Data is processed securely to generate suggestions.
  • We use industry-standard encryption for any data transmission.
  • No personal data is retained after processing.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your data may be stored in your personal iCloud account. This is governed by Apple's privacy policy and your iCloud settings. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Notifications

GetBriefed may send local notifications (such as birthday reminders) if you enable them. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not require any data transmission.

Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • Apple's built-in device encryption
  • Secure on-device storage (SwiftData)
  • No external server communication for core features

Children's Privacy

GetBriefed does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is intended for general audiences.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

support@getbriefed.app

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information. You can delete all app data at any time by deleting the app from your device.
